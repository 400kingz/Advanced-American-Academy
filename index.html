Our Programs

What We Provide

  • Advanced Restorative

    The Advanced Restorative course provides the learner with the techniques and procedures required to perform a range of advanced dental treatments, including implant restorations, veneers, and crowns.

  • Orthodontics

    Our Orthodontics course covers the fundamentals of orthodontic diagnosis and treatment planning. It also includes instruction on fixed and removable appliances, clear aligner therapy, and orthognathic surgery.

  • Periodontics

    Our Periodontics course provides in-depth knowledge of the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of periodontal disease. It also covers surgical and non-surgical periodontal therapy, as well as implantology.

  • Endodontics

    Our Endodontics course covers the diagnosis and treatment of diseases and injuries that affect the pulp and root of teeth. It includes instruction on root canal therapy, retreatment, and surgery.

  • Dental Implant Procedures and Techniques

    Missing teeth can be uncomfortable and affect your confidence. But did you know that dental implants can help restore your smile and improve your oral health?
